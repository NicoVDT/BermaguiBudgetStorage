FAQ

Common questions.

Can I get to my container any time?

Yes. You have 24/7 access through the keypad gate. There are no lights on site, so bring a torch if you're coming after dark.

What can I store?

General household and business goods: furniture, tools, boxes, bikes, trailers and most vehicles. No hazardous, perishable or illegal goods. High-value or sentimental items aren't recommended as insurance isn't included.

How do payments work?

Always in advance, by bank transfer or cash. One month upfront to start, then fortnightly or monthly, whatever suits. You're welcome to pay as far ahead as you like.

How much notice do I give when moving out?

14 days. If we're full at the time, we keep a waitlist for the next person, so the more notice the better.

Is my stuff insured?

No, goods are stored at your own risk. If you'd like cover, arrange it with your own insurer; many home and contents policies can extend to storage.
